About

I approach hair with care and structure.

My work begins with listening — closely and without assumption. From there, I refine and guide, creating a plan that reflects both your vision and what your hair can support long-term.

I don't chase trends at the expense of integrity. Healthy hair is the foundation of everything I do.

Over time, the result is consistency. Hair that feels predictable. Manageable. Aligned with your lifestyle. Less effort. More ease.

I hold a high standard for the experience I provide. That means clear communication, honest expectations, and work that prioritizes longevity. It also means mutual respect — the best outcomes happen when there is trust, when you feel comfortable being honest, and open to guidance on how to move forward.

Most clients come in thinking they need to change their hair. More often, what they need is a better understanding of how to work with it.

If that resonates, we'll work well together.
