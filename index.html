On the work

Hair doesn't need to be complicated to be effective.

Most people have been given too much information — and not enough clarity. I offer structure. A way of working with your hair that is intentional, sustainable, and aligned.

Approach

I don't believe in overriding your hair.

More often, the work is in refining it.

Through consultation, I translate what you're seeing and what's realistic — so we can move forward with clarity.

From there, I guide.

The goal is not just how your hair looks when you leave, but how it holds up in your day-to-day.

What to expect

This is collaborative — but not directionless.

You can expect:

  • Clear communication
  • Realistic timelines and pricing
  • A focus on long-term results

In return, I ask that you come:

  • with a sense of direction
  • open to guidance
  • ready to maintain what we build

Good hair is built through consistency.

Fit

For the open, the self-aware, the willing.

I'm best suited for clients who are open, self-aware, and willing to invest in the ongoing care of their hair.

If you're looking for transformation without maintenance, or results that compromise integrity, I may not be the right fit.
