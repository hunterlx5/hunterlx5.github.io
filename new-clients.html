Before you book

A sense of direction.

Come with:

  • inspiration photos
  • an understanding of what you've liked and not liked in the past
  • an openness to guidance

You don't need to have all the answers. But a sense of direction allows us to use our time intentionally.

Your first appointment

We begin with consultation.

This is where we:

  • define your goals
  • assess your current hair
  • align on timing, maintenance, and investment

From there, I create a plan — whether that's achieved in one session or built over time.

There is no rush to force a result that your hair cannot sustainably support.

On investment

Priced hourly.

$175/ hour

Most first appointments fall within:

  • 2–4+ hours depending on scope

Exact timing and pricing are confirmed during consultation.

On results

Not delivered in a single visit.

The outcome you're looking for is not created in a single visit.

It is built through:

  • consistency
  • maintenance
  • and a clear plan moving forward

My role is to guide and execute. Maintaining the result is shared.

After your appointment

Stable, rather than reactive.

To support your hair between visits:

  • a consistent product routine is recommended
  • future appointments are typically scheduled in advance

This allows your hair to remain stable, rather than reactive.

Fit

Clarity is something we can build together.

I work best with clients who:

  • communicate clearly
  • are open to guidance
  • value long-term results over quick fixes

If you're unsure of your direction, that's okay — clarity is something we can build together.

If you're looking for immediate transformation without maintenance, or results that compromise the integrity of your hair, I may not be the right fit.
